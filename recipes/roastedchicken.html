Roasted Chicken

This oven roasted chichen is delicous and easy to make. It's perfect for weekend and holidays. You can add any vegitables roasted along with the chicken

Ingredient

  • 1 whole chicken, glibets removed
  • 1/2 cup Kosher salt, 1 tbsp black pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp. onion powder
  • 1 stalk celery, leaves removed, to fill the chicken cavity
  • 1 lemon to fill the chicken cavity
  • 1 lb mini potatoes
  • 1/2 lb baby carrots
  • 1/2 bunches fresh rosemary

Steps

  1. Spread kosher salt and black pepper mixture all over the chicken, and massage the chicken.
  2. Set the seasoned chicken aside for 15 minutes, and preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  3. Place the chicken in a roasting pan, and freely place potatoes, baby carrots, and rosemary around the chicken.
  4. Bake uncovered for 1 hour and 20 minutes in the oven, to a minimum internal temperature of 180 degree(82 degree C).
  5. Remove the chicken from oven, and baste with melted margarine and dipppings. Cover with aluminum foil, and allow to rest about 20 minutes before serving.
