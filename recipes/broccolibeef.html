Broccoli Beef

This broccoli beef is delicous and easy to make. You can make it at home with simple ingredients in about 30 minues to skip the takeout. It's good to be served with rice or noodle

Ingredient

  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tsp. brown sugar
  • 1 tsp. Kosher salt
  • 1 tsp. cornstrach
  • 2 tbsp. olive oil
  • 1 tsp blalck pepper
  • 2 cup fresh boccoli crowns
  • 1 lb. sirloin steak, sliced thinly against grain
  • Thinly chopped green onion, for garnish

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk 1/4 cup soy sauce, 2 teaspoon brown sugar, and 1 teaspoon cornstrach until mixed well.
  2. Add steak season with salt and pepper and toss until steak is coated. Marinate for 15 minutes
  3. In a large skillet over medium high heat, heat oil. Add steak in a single layer for 2 minutes and then flip the layer to heat it for 2 minutes.
  4. Stir fry the beef for 1 minutes, add broccoli to the skillet and stir fry beef with broccoli for 2 minutes.
  5. Turn off the heat, remove the mixture from the skillet to a serving plate, and garnish with green onoins before serving
